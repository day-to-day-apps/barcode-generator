Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Controller and contact

Barcode Generator is operated by Day to Day Apps. Privacy enquiries can be sent to daytodayapps.contact@gmail.com.

2. Barcode generation

Basic barcode generation, visual settings and file export run locally in your browser. Entered barcode values are not sent to our server merely to generate an image.

3. Accounts and Supabase

If you create an account, Supabase processes your email address, authentication data, saved barcodes, templates, printer profiles and print jobs. These data are used to provide account features and are not sold.

4. Browser storage

We use essential local storage for theme, consent, pending saves and decoder scan history. The decoder history remains in your browser unless you export it.

5. Analytics and advertising

Google Analytics and Google AdSense are loaded only where permitted and after the required consent. Advertising remains disabled until valid AdSense configuration is available. You can change your consent by clearing site data in your browser.

6. Retention and your rights

Account data are retained while the account exists. You can export your data, update your email or password, and permanently delete the account from the account dashboard. You may also request access, correction or deletion by email.

7. Service providers

We use Cloudflare for hosting and security, Supabase for authentication and database storage, and Google for consented analytics or advertising. Each provider may process technical information under its own privacy terms.
